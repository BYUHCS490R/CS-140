What is Rush Hour about?

Rush Hour stars Jackie Chan as inspector Lee and Chris Tucker as officer Carter. The movie genre is comedy, action, adventure and mystery. The movie is directed by Brett Ratner and was released in 1998.

Rush hour is about a Chinese diplomat who's daughter was kidnapped in Los Angeles and he calls inpector Lee to help the FBI with the case. But the FBI doesn't want inspector Lee to help them and ask an LAPD officer named Carter to watch over him and direct him off the case. Even though Lee and Carter can't stand each other, they choose to work together to solve the case on their own when they figure out they've been ditched by both the FBI and police.
