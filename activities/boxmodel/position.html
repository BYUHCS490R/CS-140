Box Model

What is the difference between block tags and inline tags?

Block tags take up the full width of the screen while inline tags only take up as much width as necessary and is on the same line.

What is the margin, border, and padding?

Padding is the space on the inside of the box, the margin is for the space outside the box while the border is the edge of the box.

What are units of measurement you can use in CSS?

Pixels and percentages.

When would you use a figure tag?

When you want to add a caption to an image.
