The form tag has an "action" attribute. What does it do?

It specifies where to send the data when the form is submitted.

The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?

It specifies the HTTP method (GET or POST) to be used when submitting the form data.

What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?

With GET, the data will be displayed in the url; while post doesn't display the data in the url. POST=change, add, update, remove & GET=reading.
