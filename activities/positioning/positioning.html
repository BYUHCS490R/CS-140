Positioning

What is normal flow in HTML?

It's the default way elements are arranged on a page before any CSS is applied.

What does float do? Why do you need to clear the float?

Float moves the image to the right or the left of the page.

What do absolute, fixed, and relative do?

Absolute is placed based on the nearest parent that has a set position. Fixed positions don't move even if the page is scrolled. Relative allows us to slide the content around.

How can you make nice navigation buttons for mobile devices?

By making the display a block or inline block.
