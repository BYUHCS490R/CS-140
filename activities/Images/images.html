Images

What kind of photos are good to encode using JPEG?

It's good at compressing photos with lots of colors and gradients, like landscapes or portraits.

What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

It's best for simple images with limited colors, like logos or animations.

What is unique about the PNG format?

PNG supports lossless compression and transparency, making it great for images that need to maintain quality and have transparent backgrounds

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

You can use photo editing software to resize the image to the desired dimensions before uploading it to our website.

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

Resizing with photo editing software maintains image quality and reduces file size, leading to faster load times and better performance on the website.
