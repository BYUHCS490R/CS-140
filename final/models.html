Tesla Models

Model 3

Model 3 is the most afforable and most selling model. It's designed to be efficient, safe and everyday practicality. It focuses on ride quality and reliability with a minimalist design, keeping the price low without sacrificing quality.

Model S

Model S is a premium all-electric luxury sedan. It has really quick acceleration and offers some of the longest driving ranges in the electric vehicle market. The inside has a minimalistic design and autopilot capabilities. It's build for people who want both performance and a smooth ride.

Model Y

Model Y is compact electric SUV. It has lots of storage space both in front and in the back. It has an optional third row seating, long range drive and high efficiency which makes it more suitable for families.

Cybertruck

The Cybertruck is a futuristic pickup truck built with a stainless-steel exoskeleton for strength and durability. It has high towing and payload capabilities, fast acceleration, long range and all terrain drive.

Model X

Model X is a luxury electric SUV known for its distinctive falcon-wing doors. It offers spacious seating for up to seven passengers, high performance, and advanced safety features. It's designed for those who want a combination of luxury, space, and electric power.
